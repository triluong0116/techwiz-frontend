Introduce

Persol Corporation


"See Better, Look Better"

“Looking Clear, Perfectly Beautiful” is what we always try to bring to our guests at Persol.

The time when glasses frames were only for people with refractive errors, glasses frames just needed to see clearly are long gone. Nowadays, frames not only have to be CHEAP, but also need to be BEAUTIFUL and above all, COMFORTABLE.

That's why, from the very first days of its establishment, Persol has always tried to give its guests A NEW EXPERIENCE with:

  • A collection of stylish frames that can fit near-sighted glasses.
  • Genuine lenses with various eye protection features.
  • Aesthetic eye fitting technique for people with high myopia.

All Person facilities are equipped with high standard ophthalmology and measuring technology room

We always support our customers with free eye exams for the first time and monthly eye exams. At Persol, you will have the most comprehensive visual experience ever.

Beside that, we always help you find the best and most suitable frames for you. The right lenses with anti-glare, water-resistant, vapor/dust-proof features help you see more comfortably. And most of all, Persol always accompanies you throughout the process of using frames with a lifetime warranty service.
